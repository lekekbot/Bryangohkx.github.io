Project INC

Project Description

This project was done as part of our core modules, Project INC.
The ISO 9001 Quality Management System (QMS) is documentation an organisation does to ensure its operations and policies to maintain or improve its products and services to bolster its customer experience and to build its growth culture of continued improvement. The team aims to streamline the process of documenting ISO records for Associates Consulting company by digitalising the process.
